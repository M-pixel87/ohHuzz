Doggies

I’ve never had a pet myself, though my brother Higinio once had a cat. Sometimes I wonder if I’d need a pet out in the world as well I worry that a person might start to see themselves as my pet.. Like I’m the one deciding what they eat or that they feel the need to stay close, like a loyal doggie. But that’s only because what I want in a pet is different from what I value in a person. I appreciate a pet’s loyalty and comfort. But with a person, I admire their understanding and free will to choose to stay, to be a companion not out of obedience, but out of the want to stuggle together although i might be hard to deal with.

Apple Pie

My apple pie is a unique dish, a feeling that no other will do, though other plates may be just as appealing. The craving for it is strange to me like something from a list of recipes or a flock of sheep it feels incomplete without it. I know that if I were to drop my apple pie, another dish could take its place, but I would still remember it: the sweetness, warmth, and the slight burn id sometimes leave in my mouth when i was caught off guard (RBF whipped ..+1). The apple pie doesn’t make me the cook or give meaning to my existence in the kitchen, but it’s certainly a recipe I hope never poisons my heart and continues to keep me company at the table.
